Phase 1 of the experiment will now begin. You will take turns being either drawer or guesser. In Phase 1, six sentences will be shown.

When your role is drawer, one sentence will be highlighted in green. Your task is to make a drawing to communicate the highlighted sentence to the guesser. Please do NOT use any letters, words, or numbers while drawing. The aim is to make a drawing that the guesser will be able to successfully distinguish from the other sentences. When you are satisfied with your drawing, simply click the ‘Done’-button to send the drawing to the guesser.

When your role is guesser, you simply have to wait until the drawer sends you a drawing. When you receive a drawing, please click on the the sentence you think matches the drawing and press the select button. You will be notified whether your guess was right or wrong.

After each round the roles of drawer and guesser will be switched. This setup will be repeated throughout all three phases of the experiment.
